Disability Support Services

At Victorian Health and Support Care, we believe that everyone deserves to live a fulfilling and independent life, regardless of disability. That's why we offer a wide range of disability support services to meet the needs of individuals and families.

We also offer specialized services for people with specific disabilities, such as:

  • ⚪ Visual impairment services such as orientation and mobility training, low vision rehabilitation, and adaptive technology training
  • ⚪ Hearing impairment services such as sign language instruction, auditory training, and assistive listening devices
  • ⚪ Intellectual and developmental disability services such as life skills training, behavioral support, and social skills development
  • ⚪ Physical and mobility disability services such as physical therapy, occupational therapy, and adaptive equipment training

We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized services that meet the unique needs of each individual we support. We work closely with our clients and their families to develop individualized care plans that help them achieve their goals and live their best lives.

If you or someone you know is in need of disability support services, please contact us today. We would be happy to answer your questions and help you get started.

Why Choose Victorian Health and Support Care

  • ⬛ We are experienced and qualified. Our staff members are highly trained and experienced in providing disability support services.
  • ⬛ We are committed to quality. We are dedicated to providing our clients with the highest quality of care and services possible.
  • ⬛ We are individualized. We take the time to get to know each individual client and develop a personalized care plan that meets their unique needs.
  • ⬛ We are affordable. We offer a variety of affordable payment options to make our services accessible to everyone.

Victorian Health and Support Care is a registered NDIS Provider that is committed to supporting people with disabilities to achieve their aspirations and live full and inclusive lives in the community.
