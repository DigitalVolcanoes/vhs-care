Victorian Health: Your Trusted Source for Support

Victorian Health and Support Care is a registered NDIS Provider that is committed to supporting people with disabilities to achieve their aspirations and live full and inclusive lives in the community. We provide highly personal, responsive, and participant-focused services that are tailored to meet the individual needs of each person we support.

We employ highly competent and qualified staff who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of people with disabilities. We work closely with participants and their families to develop and implement individualized support plans that promote dignity, integrity, and respect.

Our Best Services

Victorian Health and Support Care provides a range of innovative and personalized services to help people with disabilities live their best lives. From in-home support to community participation, we offer everything you need to achieve your goals and live independently in the community. At Victorian Health and Support Care, we believe in the power of community.

Independence is at the core of our philosophy. Through our range of services, we empower individuals to lead fulfilling lives on their terms. We provide the necessary tools and encouragement to navigate daily challenges with confidence. Our dedicated and compassionate care team is committed to creating a positive and supportive environment.

NDIS

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is a national scheme that provides financial assistance to people with disabilities to cover the cost of their reasonable and necessary supports. It is a lifelong scheme that is available to people of all ages and levels of disability.

NDIS will help people with disability to achieve their goals. This may include greater independence, community involvement, employment and improved well being
